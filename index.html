Research Overview

I am a PhD Researcher at Rise Lab, Queen’s University, supervised by Dr. Yuan Tian. My research focuses on Software Engineering for AI (SE4AI), particularly the reuse and integration of pre-trained models within Open-Source Software (OSS) ecosystems.

With over 10 years of experience as a Software Engineer, I bridge the gap between academic research and industrial practice in backend architecture and ML supply-chain transparency.
