About

Hello! I am Jerin Yasmin, a PhD student at Queen’s University, Canada. My research focuses on software engineering for AI (SE4AI), working on understanding and improving data and ML workflows in software systems. I am interested in AI4SE, Mining Software Repositories, and Software Maintenance and Evolution. My work has been featured in top-tier software engineering conferences such as ICSME, ICPC, and MSR. Before pursuing my graduate studies at Queen’s, I gained industry experience in backend architecture, web services development, and security enhancements.
