About

I am a PhD student at Queen’s University. My research interests include Software Engineering for AI (SE4AI), Mining Software Repositories, and Software Maintenance.

My work aims to understand the challenges developers face when implementing complex ML workflows and managing software dependencies in the era of pre-trained models.
