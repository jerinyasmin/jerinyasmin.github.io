About

News: Our paper on Maven Ecosystem dynamics was accepted to MSR 2025!

Hello! I am Jerin Yasmin, a PhD student at Queen’s University, Canada. My research focuses on software engineering for AI (SE4AI), specifically understanding and improving data and ML workflows.

I am passionate about AI4SE, Mining Software Repositories, and Software Maintenance. Previously, I gained industry experience in backend architecture and security enhancements.
