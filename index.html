About Me

I am a PhD student at Queen’s University, Canada. My research focuses on software engineering for AI (SE4AI), working on understanding and improving data and ML workflows in software systems. I am interested in AI4SE, Mining Software Repositories, and Software Maintenance and Evolution.

Before pursuing my graduate studies at Queen’s, I gained extensive industry experience in backend architecture, web services development, and security enhancements.
